The SU2 International Developers Society (IDS) is ready!
Our journey will begin on 5/26/2018 @ 7am PST.


If you believe that numerical simulation is an essential part of our technological world and, it should be free and open for everyone... SU2 IDS is your professional society!

With your support, IDS will start shaping the open-source community of SU2 contributors to recognize, connect, inspire, and expand the worldwide team of SU2 enthusiasts. We strongly believe that, all together, the SU2 open-source community has the potential to revolutionize numerical simulation, the way it is developed, and its impact on the society.

The objective of our first meeting is to introduce the current leadership team, the open membership system and, future initiatives.
